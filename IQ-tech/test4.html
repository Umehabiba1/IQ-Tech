1. Prepare
  • Identifying your organization's goals, priorities, and risk tolerance.
  • Defining the scope of the CISO's role and responsibilities within your organization.
2. Analyze
  • Assessing your current cybersecurity posture.
  • Identifying vulnerabilities, threats, and evaluating the effectiveness of your existing security controls.
3. Implement
  • Developing and implementing cybersecurity policies, procedures, and protocols.
  • Deploying security technologies and solutions to mitigate risks.
4. Monitor
  • Continuous monitoring of systems, networks, and data for suspicious activities or anomalies.
  • Performing regular security audits and assessments.
5. Respond
  • Developing an incident response plan to address security incidents.
  • Responding promptly to security breaches or incidents to minimize damage and recover.
6. Review
  • Conducting regular reviews and evaluations of your cybersecurity program.
  • Identifying areas for improvement and implementing necessary changes.
